The BioCook Process
The Carbon Product

A clean renewable alternative to fossil fuels, and more

The High-Energy Carbon produced by our BioCook® Technology can be used as a clean alternative to fossil fuels, with benefits from above 5000 Kcal/kg of heat value and clean combustion.

Because our product is from carbon absorbed by recently harvested biomass, not fossil fuels, our process does not add greenhouse gases and is carbon neutral.

The BioCook® product has high carbon content and is hydrophobic for easy drying. With a heat value of 4000-6000kcal/kg it is equivalent to thermal coal but with far less ash, sulphur and other pollutants.

However, unlike coal, our products does not come from fossil fuels. The carbon dioxide emitted during the combustion process is the same as the carbon dioxide emitted during the natural decay of the organic material. Because we humans are always making waste, our carbon product is a fuel that is fully sustainable without increasing greenhouse gas emissions.

Additional products and applications

The product is readily usable as a fuel source, however, there is a wide variety of other uses.

It can be used directly as a soil amendment. Providing carbon, nutrients and a structure on which beneficial micro-organisms can grow and help with irrigation and fetilizing.

The BioCook® process reuses the liquid fraction, however, excess liquid and gas generated are used to create a liquid fertilizer.

With further processing, the carbon product can be converted into activated carbon, a syngass, chemical raw ingredients, or as a carbon source for other industries.
