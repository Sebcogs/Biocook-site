The BioCook Process
Converts waste into fuel

Carbon is the building blocks of all organic materials on Earth.


BioCook® is an internationally innovative technology that recycles waste into value-added products. We convert waste materials that contain high content of carbon and water, such as food waste from kitchens and restaurants, food-processing and beverage industries, urban and rural garbage and agri-biomass wastes (e.g., crop stalks and animal manure) into valuable clean energy product (High-Energy Carbon ) and completely destroy hazardous material such as antibiotics and pathogens, achieving the transformation of waste-to-value and resources.

The High-Energy Carbon produced by our BioCook® Technology can be used as a clean alternative to fossil fuels, with benefits of above 5000 KCal/kg of heat value and clean combustion. High-Energy Carbon can also be used as a soil amendment, significantly improving the holding capacities of water and fertilizer in soil.

In addition to realizing sustainable carbon sequestration, BioCook® technology can create significant economical, environmental, and social benefits.
