Company Information

History

2014
  • The main operating company Zhuhai HuiDong is established and begins operating the first BioCook Pilot Plant in Zhuhai, Guangdong.
2015-2016
  • Upgraded the Pilot Plant to a Small Scale Demonstration Facility
  • Implemented multiple Reaction Vessels for continuous operation
  • Reduced operating cost by using patent-pending heat recovery technologies
  • Filed several patents in China
2017
  • Retained Sedin Engineering Co., Ltd. for process design and equipment selection for BioCook® Technology based on Chinese GB standards.
  • Entered into formal agreement with Wuyuan County government for collaboration
  • Construction begins on the 1st BioCook® facility in the world with 100 ton daily processing capacity in Nei Meng Gu.
2018
  • 2 patents approved with 3 still pending
  • Small demonstration facility begins operation in NeiMengGu while large facility construction continues
