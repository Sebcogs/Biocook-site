The BioCook Process
How garbage can become valuable

BioCook® technology surpasses all other waste treatment methods on the market.

This technology is simple and robust to changing feed material and provide a new environmentally friendly solution to organic waste problems.

Our technology is based on Hydrothermal Carbonization (HTC) first developed in 1913. Nature performs the carbonization of organic matter into coal over millions of years. This HTC technology accomplishes much the same in only a few hours.

BioCook® technology provides improved waste treatment, environmentally clean solutions and societal benefits above competitors.

The technology and process have been tested and verified through pilot tests. The equipment manufacturing and design for industrialization of BioCook technology have been achieved.

The worlds first large scale garbage treatment plant will become operational in 2018. It will generate high-energy carbon solid product from domestic garbage and cut back or eliminate pollution.
