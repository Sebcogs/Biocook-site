The BioCook Process
Converts waste into fuel

HuiDong Corp Inc (HD Corp) is commercializing a developed and demonstrated proprietary bio-waste to biofuel process. This BioCook technology surpasses existing biomass conversion technologies such as garbage incinerator, gasification and torrefaction by yielding higher end product energy density, lowering costs and accepting a wider range of wet feedstocks with no harmful emissions.

HD Corp has recently finished a Pilot Scale plant which is successfully converting MSW, garden waste, animal manure and wood waste into high energy density Cumulative Carbon fuel.

Our BioCoook process, Cumulative Carbon product and preliminary engineering design has been independently assessed by several engineering and environmental groups which verify our commercial viability.
